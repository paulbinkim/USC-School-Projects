Final Project Proposal // Stock Market

Topic

For my final project, I would like to create a website about the stock market. Specifically, I would create a well-designed interface that lists the sectors of the stock market, and provide example stocks that are included within those respective sectors.

Audience

My intended audience is for those interested in the stock market.

Design

My design will be very clean and sleek (user-friendly). For colors, I will stick with a calm theme of greens and colors that complement green (Not only limited to the colors listed).

Tea Green
Light Moss Green
Light Blue

Inspiration

*Inititally, I would like to use the format and minimalistic design of these websites, and make use of a navigation system respectively.

  • Smartdriving
  • Letter
  • Revolve

Scope

I am planning to have around 12 pages to my website.

One page would be for the Home Screen, and the following pages (through a navigation bar) would be the different sectors of the stock market: Materials, Industrials, Financials, Energy, Consumer Discretionary, Information Technology, Communication Services, Real Estate, Health Care, Consumer Staples, Utility. Within those pages of sectors, I intend to list 5-7 example stocks of the respective sector with an image and general information.
